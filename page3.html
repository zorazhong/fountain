Fountain Biopharma Inc. ＆ Synovel Sciences Inc. Announced to Merge

in Press Conference

    The board of directors decided to merge Fountain Biopharma Inc. & Synovel Sciences Inc. and held a press

conference at the counter trading center on January 9th, the press release reads as follows:


   Fountain Biopharma Inc. is a world-class antibody new drug company, focused on innovative development of

humanized monoclonal antibodies for treating allergic disorders and cancers. It has successfully sinstituted several antibody library, high yield production cell line, upstream/downstream process development technology, analytical methods development and ILAC/TAF certified aminal facility. Pipelines and key technologies are strengthened by

colloaboration with several leading academic institutes. Fountain has four pipelines in clinical and pre-clinical stages. Global drug development strategy and well positioned as a platform to enter into Asia Pacific markets make

Fountain to be a great partner in the development of antibody therapeutics.


  Synovel Sciences Inc. is a new company dedicated to the development of new drugs for DNA Aptamer and a unique platform for stem cell technology for lung cancer. It has introduced DNA innovations from exclusive research

institutes such as the Central Research Institute and the National Taiwan University. Drug and chemical small

molecule research and development achievements, including anti-PD1, PD-L1 and CTLA-4 of small molecules of cancer drugs and lung cancer stem cell drug screening platform. These platforms not only support the development of cancer therapies, but also explore new targets for novel tumors, which could be used to develop innovative

antitumor drugs worldwide.


  In order to expand the scale of operations, strengthen the R & D community of new drugs, enhance the

competitiveness of the international market and other strategic development goals, the two companies today (January 9, 106) Board of Directors held a merger case. Fountain Biopharma Inc.(code: 4159) was the survival company and


   Synovel Sciences Inc was eradicated company, the name of the surviving company after the merger is Fountain

Biopharma Inc. The temporarily convertible ratio of Fountain Biopharma Inc. & Synovel Sciences Inc. is 1: 0.8787.


   The proposal will be discussed at the interim meeting of shareholders held on February 24th, and approved by the competent authority. The consolidation date is tentatively set at March 26th. After the merger, the paid-in capital of Fountain Biopharma Inc. will be approximately 3 billion NT dollars. Through the integration and management of the complementary characteristics of the two sides, the talent and resources will be shared and the international clinical development and authorization will be fully developed to create the greatest interests for the shareholders.
